About the Firm

Messrs Bila Salehuddin & Co.

Peguambela & Peguamcara · Advocates & Solicitors
Registered with the Malaysian Bar — Reg. No. 000020008177

Established in 2024, Bila Salehuddin & Co. has swiftly positioned itself as a reputable and forward-looking law firm with its Principal Office in Perak Darul Ridzuan and a growing presence in Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur.

The firm is led by one Principal Partner, supported by two experienced Consultants and two dedicated Legal Assistants, forming a dynamic legal team that combines depth of experience with modern legal insight. Backed by a capable support staff of eight skilled paralegals and clerical personnel, we ensure efficiency, accuracy, and client satisfaction in every engagement.

Bila Salehuddin & Co. offers a full range of legal services with a primary focus on Conveyancing, Commercial Law, and Litigation. Our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and a client-centric approach distinguishes us as a trusted partner in the Malaysian legal landscape.

Our approach

We started Bila Salehuddin & Co. on a simple idea: clients deserve a lawyer who answers the question they actually asked — in language they can act on. No padding, no posturing.

We act across multiple disciplines because we believe in doing them well. Eviction work demands urgency, procedure, and clear strategy. Contract work demands attention to detail and a sense of what each clause is really for. Both reward focus. Clients work directly with the lawyer handling their matter — there are no layers between the advice you need and the person giving it.

Where we practise

Our principal office is in Ipoh, Perak Darul Ridzuan, with a branch office in Batu Caves, W.P. Kuala Lumpur. We are admitted to the Malaysian Bar and act for clients across the Klang Valley, Perak, and throughout Peninsular Malaysia.

Intan Nabila Binti Salehuddin

Managing Partner · Principal Partner

Intan Nabila is a distinguished legal professional who graduated with Second Upper Class Honours from the University of the West of England. Following her academic achievements, she pursued and successfully obtained her Certificate in Legal Practice in Malaysia, demonstrating her commitment to excellence in the legal field.

Admitted to the Bar in 2023, Intan Nabila honed her skills in the service sector for a year, gaining invaluable experience through her chambering and early practice. Her passion for law and dedication to her clients inspired her to establish Bila Salehuddin & Co., where she now serves as the Principal Partner.

Intan Nabila's expertise extends to various areas of law, but she is particularly renowned for her specialisation in accident and personal injury cases. As the Person-In-Charge (PIC) for such matters, she brings a meticulous and compassionate approach, ensuring that each client receives personalised attention and the best possible outcome.
